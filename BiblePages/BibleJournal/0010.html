Added Law: Thou Shalt Love Thy Neighbour, And Hate Thine Enemy.
Ley Añadida: Amarás á tu prójimo, y aborrecerás á tu enemigo.

English: This so far is an eye catch, or an eye opener to how Loving God is, that is being spoken by Jesus Himself, for us to folllow His God Father's heart. It is a long written instruction from Matthew 5: 43-48 but something great above it Jesus emphasized. As written in Bible Journal #1 of "The Golden GIFT of GOD is His LOVE for us" it is shown in Matthew 5: 48, how JESUS want us to look at the best role model on this universe. That is how loving His Father is. This is rarely spoken or heard in some sermons, but Jesus is the one who himself, spreading to people during on His time on Jerusalem, to be as kind as God Father. As also written in Journal #1, Jesus said, why call Jesus good, for there is only one who is Good, and that is His God Father, our Father.

Spanish: Jesús revela el corazón del Padre, señalándonos al modelo supremo: el amor y la bondad perfecta de Dios. Somos llamados no solo a seguir reglas, sino a reflejar la bondad del Único que es verdaderamente Bueno. Como se escribió en el Diario Bíblico #1, Jesús enfatizó que solo hay uno que es Bueno, y ese es Su Dios Padre. Él nos anima a ser tan bondadosos como el Padre, quien es el mejor modelo a seguir en el universo.

Matthew 5: 43-48 Mateo 5: 43-48
43 Ye have heard that it hath been said, Thou shalt love thy neighbour, and hate thine enemy.
43 Oísteis que fué dicho: Amarás á tu prójimo, y aborrecerás á tu enemigo.
44 But I say unto you, Love your enemies, bless them that curse you, do good to them that hate you, and pray for them which despitefully use you, and persecute you;
44 Mas yo os digo: Amad á vuestros enemigos, bendecid á los que os maldicen, haced bien á los que os aborrecen, y orad por los que os ultrajan y os persiguen;
45 That ye may be the children of your Father which is in heaven: for he maketh his sun to rise on the evil and on the good, and sendeth rain on the just and on the unjust.
45 Para que seáis hijos de vuestro Padre que está en los cielos: que hace que su sol salga sobre malos y buenos, y llueve sobre justos é injustos.
46 For if ye love them which love you, what reward have ye? do not even the publicans the same?
46 Porque si amareis á los que os aman, ¿qué recompensa tendréis? ¿no hacen también lo mismo los publicanos?
47 And if ye salute your brethren only, what do ye more than others ? do not even the publicans so?
47 Y si abrazareis á vuestros hermanos solamente, ¿qué hacéis de más? ¿no hacen también así los Gentiles?
48 Be ye therefore perfect, even as your Father which is in heaven is perfect.
48 Sed, pues, vosotros perfectos, como vuestro Padre que está en los cielos es perfecto.

English: This is so far, an eye opening, where in, if God is loving to evil and good people. We should be the same compassionate towards others, that Jesus emphasized of it is the HEART of HIS GOD FATHER. It is the core personality of God to be loving to evil and good. And we should open our eyes, our hearts, in being understanding, and kind, and compassionate, not only to those who love us, but to those who hate us, and those who seems evil to us. But God don't easily judge, as shown by Jesus, but His Father God is compassionate.
