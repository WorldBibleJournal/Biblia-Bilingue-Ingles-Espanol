Added Law: Give To Him That Asketh Thee And From Him That Would Borrow Of Thee Turn Not Thou Away.

English: This is a sudden cut off of the do not hate your neighbor, but the next journal will speak back of hatred. But this one speaks of the not only don't hate your neighbor, but give what you can for them. Do not turn them away when they seek help from you. Financially, or mentally or physically. As in this day and age, especially in the Philippines, people are extremely giving in the Philppines, but we are being taught, to sometimes learn how to speak no to others. To disregard giving anyone who is asking and seeking help, but Jesus said otherwise below in Matthew 5: 42.

Spanish: Hay un corte repentino en el tema de no odiar al prójimo, pero el siguiente diario volverá a hablar del odio. Este, en cambio, trata de que no solo no debemos odiar al prójimo, sino de darles lo que podamos. No les des la espalda cuando busquen tu ayuda, ya sea financiera, mental o físicamente. En los tiempos actuales, especialmente en Filipinas, la gente es extremadamente generosa; sin embargo, se nos enseña que a veces debemos aprender a decir que no a los demás y a ignorar a quien pide ayuda. Pero Jesús dice lo contrario en Mateo 5:42.

Matthew 5: 42 Mateo 5: 42
42 Give to him that asketh thee, and from him that would borrow of thee turn not thou away.
42 Al que te pidiere, dale; y al que quisiere tomar de ti prestado, no se lo rehuses.

English: Next in Bible Journal #10, is the continuation about hatred towards people, and not only be kind to those who are kind to you, but also to those who hate you.

Spanish: A continuación, en el Diario Bíblico #10, continuamos hablando sobre el odio; se nos enseña a no ser buenos únicamente con quienes son buenos con nosotros, sino también con aquellos que nos odian.
