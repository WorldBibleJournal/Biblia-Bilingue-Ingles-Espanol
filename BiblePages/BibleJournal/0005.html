English: In Bible Journal #3, we spoke about Ten Commandments Are Still in Full Effect, and here, we spoke about what Jesus has added in the ten commandments. In Bible Journal #4 we spoke of the Thou Shalt Not Kill. In this day and age, is where a lot of problem persist.
