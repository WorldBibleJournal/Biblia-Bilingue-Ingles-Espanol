18 #7

English: In Bible Journal #3, we spoke about Ten Commandments Are Still in Full Effect, and here, we spoke about what Jesus has added in the ten commandments. In Bible Journal #5 we spoke about the adultery, and in here we talk about Divorce, that is then written on Matthew 5: 31-32, but after reading that, there are other parts in the bible where it spoke of divorce and marriage, that will be written below after the first spoken verses.

Spanish: En el Diario Bíblico #3, hablamos de que los Diez Mandamientos Siguen en Pleno Vigor, y aquí hablamos de lo que Jesús ha añadido a los diez mandamientos. En el Diario Bíblico #5, hablamos sobre el adulterio, y en este apartado hablamos sobre el Divorcio, tal como está escrito en Mateo 5:31-32. Después de leer eso, encontraremos otras partes de la Biblia donde se habla del divorcio y el matrimonio, las cuales se escribirán a continuación después de los primeros versículos mencionados.

Matthew 5: 31-32 Mateo 5: 31-32
31 It hath been said, Whosoever shall put away his wife, let him give her a writing of divorcement:
31 También fué dicho: Cualquiera que repudiare á su mujer, déle carta de divorcio:
32 But I say unto you, That whosoever shall put away his wife, saving for the cause of fornication, causeth her to commit adultery: and whosoever shall marry her that is divorced committeth adultery.
32 Mas yo os digo, que el que repudiare á su mujer, fuera de causa de fornicación, hace que ella adultere; y el que se casare con la repudiada, comete adulterio.

English: And additional reference is spoken again on Luke 16: 18, of stating about the divorce and marraige.

Spanish: Y se menciona una referencia adicional nuevamente en Lucas 16:18, donde se declara sobre el divorcio y el matrimonio.

Luke 16: 18 Lucas 16: 18
18 Whosoever putteth away his wife, and marrieth another, committeth adultery: and whosoever marrieth her that is put away from her husband committeth adultery.
